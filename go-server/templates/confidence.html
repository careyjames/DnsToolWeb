Maturity Levels

Development

Fewer than 100 consecutive passing audit runs. The engine is learning.

Verified

100+ consecutive passes. Results are reliable but still maturing.

Consistent

500+ passes over 30+ days with no regressions. Production-grade accuracy.

Gold

1,000+ passes over 90+ days. Battle-tested across diverse domains.

Gold Master

5,000+ passes over 180+ days. The highest confidence tier — reference-grade intelligence.

Two-Layer Auditing

Each protocol is audited at two independent layers:

Collection Layer

Validates that raw DNS records are retrieved correctly — the right queries, the right record types, proper timeout handling.

Analysis Layer

Validates that collected data is interpreted correctly — RFC compliance checks, security assessment logic, risk classification.

Why This Matters

Most security tools grade your domain without telling you how confident they are in their own results. DNS Tool is different: the ICAE provides full transparency into our analytical accuracy.

When you see a maturity badge next to a protocol, you know exactly how many audit runs back that confidence. No black boxes. No hand-waving. Every claim backed by deterministic test cases.
